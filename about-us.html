ABOUT

Janasnehi Diagnostics is an affordable diagnostic center backed by a team of highly experienced and qualified Pathologists, Biochemists, Microbiologists, and Radiologists. We are committed to enhancing patient care through advanced, patient-centric diagnostic services, supporting clinicians in making accurate and timely decisions. Our goal is to transform diagnostic healthcare by adopting the latest technologies and clinically validated assays that ensure precision and reliability.

At Janasnehi, we focus on delivering accurate and reliable diagnostic results across various specialties including Pathology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Molecular Diagnostics, Radiodiagnosis, and Imaging at reasonable price. Our state-of-the-art laboratory, equipped with the latest technology, is designed to meet the growing demand for high-quality diagnostics, offering comprehensive services under one roof.

In today’s rapidly evolving healthcare environment, where disease patterns and healthcare needs are constantly changing, we strive to stay ahead by adapting to innovations and embracing advancements. Our commitment is to offer unique, high-end investigations and specialized services, ensuring patient satisfaction and trust.

With cutting-edge facilities and a skilled team of experts, we are capable of customizing diagnostic solutions to meet the specific needs of every patient, supporting better health outcomes through accurate diagnosis.

Our Services & Commitment

Preventive Healthcare Packages

We offer a range of preventive health check-up packages, carefully curated based on age, gender, and occupation-specific risks. Our packages aim to identify and prevent diseases early, promoting overall well-being.

Certifications and Quality Standards

Our Radiology Department is certified by the AERB and Pollution Control Board. Additionally, we participate in internationally recognized EQAS programs, ensuring our processes and results are aligned with global standards and ISO guidelines.

Continuous Innovation and Upgradation

At Janasnehi Diagnostics, we are committed to the regular upgradation of equipment and technology across all centers. All the tests are carefully designed and executed based on scientific evidence.

Employee Training and Standards

We conduct regular training sessions and meetings for our consultants and technical staff to stay updated with the latest developments in healthcare and diagnostics. Our focus is on continuous learning and improvement, ensuring that our team delivers the highest standards of diagnostic care.
